About Crisis Connect

Crisis Connect is an innovative project that integrates IoT technology with web platforms to enhance disaster response and prevention efforts.

Our main goal is to provide real-time information about disasters and emergencies, enabling swift and effective responses from disaster prevention departments.

Using a combination of GSM and GPS technologies, we ensure that accurate location data is transmitted, allowing authorities to quickly assess the situation and allocate resources efficiently.

Our Mission

At Crisis Connect, our mission is to leverage technology for the greater good by:

  • Enhancing disaster response capabilities
  • Providing real-time information to authorities
  • Empowering communities to report emergencies
  • Utilizing machine learning for predictive analysis

How It Works

  • Crisis Connect operates through a user-friendly web portal where users can register and report disasters or emergencies they encounter.

  • Upon receiving a report, our system utilizes machine learning models to analyze the data and predict whether it poses a potential threat.

  • Simultaneously, the GPS coordinates of the reported incident are sent to relevant authorities, enabling them to initiate timely response measures.
